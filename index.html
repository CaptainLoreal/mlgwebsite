About Us

A Rigorous Approach to Leadership Development

The quality of leadership determines the quality of an organisation.

Munich Leadership Group was founded on a simple conviction: that the quality of leadership determines the quality of an organisation. We exist to raise that quality — through rigorous programmes, strategic advisory, and long-term partnerships with clients who share our commitment to excellence.

Headquartered in Munich and operating internationally, we bring together an exceptional faculty of scholars, practitioners, and coaches across executive education, assessment, team development, and cultural transformation.

Research-Led

Our methodology is grounded in the latest leadership science and evidence-based practice.

Practitioner-Driven

Faculty with real-world executive experience bring immediacy and relevance to every session.

Bespoke Design

Every engagement is tailored to the specific challenges and ambitions of the organisation.

Global Perspective

A cross-cultural lens woven through all programmes to prepare leaders for international complexity.

Services

Our Programmes & Services

From open-enrolment programmes to fully bespoke corporate academies, we offer a comprehensive portfolio of leadership development solutions.

01

Executive Education

Intensive programmes designed for senior leaders — combining strategic thinking, behavioural insight, and personal development in a rigorous residential format.

02

Corporate Academies

End-to-end design and delivery of in-company leadership academies — from capability framework development to programme architecture and faculty deployment.

03

Leadership Assessment

Psychometric tools, 360° feedback processes, and structured assessment centres to identify potential, map development needs, and inform succession planning.

04

Executive Coaching

One-to-one coaching engagements with senior executives, delivered by an experienced faculty of internationally accredited coaches with deep sector knowledge.

05

Team Development

Facilitated offsites and structured team interventions that strengthen alignment, resolve conflict, and accelerate collective performance at the leadership level.

06

Cultural Transformation

Advisory and facilitation services that support organisations navigating significant change — mergers, strategic pivots, and culture-shaping initiatives.
